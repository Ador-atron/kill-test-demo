# Gen Flow - Sustainable Luxury Streetwear

## Brand Essence
- **Tagline**: "Born in the Streets, Built for the Future"
- **Mission**: Luxury streetwear at the intersection of architectural design and radical environmental responsibility
- **Target**: Eco-conscious professionals aged 25-40
- **Origin**: London-based, started in a small studio

## Design System

### Aesthetic Fusion
Blends Apple-style premium minimalism with Claude-style warm editorial approach:
- Apple: generous white space, precise typography, pill-shaped CTAs
- Claude: warm editorial feel, terracotta undertones

### Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: `#FAFAF8` (warm off-white)
- **Dark Surface**: `#1A1A1A` (near-black)
- **Sage Green Accent**: `#8B9A7D` (primary accent - sustainable)
- **Terracotta**: `#C96442` (warm editorial accent)
- **Warm Sand**: `#E8E6DC` (secondary surface)
- **Text Primary**: `#141413`
- **Text Secondary**: `#5E5D59`

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Inter/System UI, tight tracking, medium weight
- **Body**: Inter/System UI, relaxed line-height
- **Accent**: Serif for editorial moments

### Animation Philosophy
Extreme bubbly animations using CSS/spring-like transitions:
- Bouncy scale transforms on hover
- Elastic easing for reveals
- Floating/parallax effects
- Pulse micro-interactions
- Spring-based scroll animations
